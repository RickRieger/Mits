Our Product

 

MITS is a plug-in tool that allows end-users to maintain inventory and enhance the product availability, effienciency and effectiveness on third party marketplaces. MITS will allow greater accuracy for sellers and help to ensure that buyers recieve the product they intended to purchase.

 

MITS will create value to you as a customer by offering services that invigorate and renew marketplace inventory with effective and efficient use of technology.

Main Features

  • Resolves issues with multiple items with the same ASIN and quality condition (New, Like New, Very Good, Good, and Acceptable).
    •  
  • Allows for comparison of physical inventory with marketplace inventory. This allows the item you have in physical inventory to accurately match what is listed on the marketplace.

Benefits to Consumers

  • Returns are a costly expense for all retailers, with MITS fixing inventory errors, you won't have to worry about those cost anymore!
    •  
  • There could be instances where you could be competing with yourself based on repricer algorithms, but with MITS, you exclusively compete with your competitors!
    •  
  • Having inventory at the right place, time, and value is required for a modern supply chain which demands accuracy and effiency.
